Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 11, 2024

Last Updated: November 11, 2024

Welcome to Gryphon Alumni, a social networking platform designed to connect current and former Guelph Gryphons football players. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. By using Gryphon Alumni, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password
  • Profile Information: Display name, hometown, previous school, position, graduation year, program/study, awards, occupation, industry interests
  • User Content: Posts, comments, messages, photos, and other content you create or share
  • Professional Documents: Resumes or other career-related documents (optional, current players only)
  • Photos: Profile pictures, header backdrop images, and photos shared in posts

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent on app, interaction patterns
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time/date stamps
  • Crash and Diagnostic Data: Error reports and app performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and Maintain Services: Enable core features like profiles, messaging, and social feed
  • User Connections: Help you connect with other current players and alumni
  • Job Opportunities: Display job postings and career opportunities to relevant users
  • Communication: Send you notifications, updates, and respond to your inquiries
  • Improve Services: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience and app functionality
  • Security: Detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or security threats
  • Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Within the App

  • Public Profile Information: Your name, profile photo, position, program, and other profile details are visible to other verified users of the app
  • Posts and Comments: Content you share in the social feed is visible to other users
  • Direct Messages: Messages are private between you and the recipient(s)

3.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Authentication, database (Firestore), and file storage (Firebase Storage)
    • Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Expo (650 Industries, Inc.): Mobile app development and deployment platform
    • Privacy Policy: expo.dev/privacy

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as:

  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Law enforcement requests
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will provide notice before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Your data is stored securely using Firebase services, which use industry-standard security measures
  • Data is stored on servers located in the United States and may be transferred to other countries where Firebase operates
  • Profile photos and documents are stored in Firebase Storage with secure access controls

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Secure authentication using Firebase Authentication
  • Access controls and authentication requirements for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and updates

Important: While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Update

  • You can access and update your profile information at any time through the app settings
  • You can edit or delete your posts and comments

5.2 Data Deletion

  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Upon account deletion, your profile and content will be removed, though some data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes
  • Messages you've sent to other users may remain in their message history

5.3 Communication Preferences

  • You can manage notification settings within the app
  • You can opt out of certain communications, though we may still send you essential service-related messages

6. Children's Privacy

Gryphon Alumni is intended for current and former university football players, who are typically 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 13 years of age. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your information as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: After account deletion, we may retain certain data for up to 90 days for backup purposes, and longer if required by law
  • Legal Compliance: We may retain information as necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ. By using Gryphon Alumni, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy within the app
  • Sending an in-app notification or email
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of Gryphon Alumni after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

12. Analytics and Advertising

Currently, Gryphon Alumni does not use third-party analytics or advertising services beyond those provided by Firebase and Expo. If this changes, we will update this policy accordingly.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • App Name: Gryphon Alumni
  • Organization: Guelph Gryphons Football
  • Email: support@gryphonalumni.com
  • Response Time: We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days

For account deletion requests, please include your registered email address and a brief description of your request.

14. Consent

By using Gryphon Alumni, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described herein.
